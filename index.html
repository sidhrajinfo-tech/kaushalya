🎓 About Our Platform

About Kaushalya Academy

An independent technical learning platform created to make exam practice structured, practical and accessible on both mobile and desktop.

Learning built for serious technical exam preparation

Kaushalya Academy helps students prepare for technical and competitive examinations through chapter-wise MCQ practice, previous-year questions and full-length mock tests. The learning flow is designed to help students revise important concepts, recognise repeated question patterns and understand their performance after every attempt.

Practice with purpose

Questions are organised around useful topics and frequently tested concepts instead of random practice.

Simple learning flow

Learners can move from topic practice to PYQs and then to exam-style mock tests without unnecessary complexity.

Performance awareness

Instant results and review tools help students identify mistakes and decide what they should revise next.

Our content principles

  • Practice material should support learning, revision and realistic exam preparation.
  • Explanations should help learners understand and correct mistakes.
  • Course information, pricing and access details should remain clear.
  • The platform should be fast, mobile friendly and easy to navigate.
  • Learning material should be reviewed and improved as the question library grows.
Independent platform: Kaushalya Academy is not an official website of GPSC, GSSSB, PSI Wireless, ISRO, GATE, RRB, SSC, DRDO or any other examination authority, government department or public-sector organisation. Examination names are used only to describe the type of preparation material available.
🔒 Privacy & Data

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information may be collected when you use Kaushalya Academy and how that information may be handled.

1. Information we may collect

Depending on the feature used, the platform may receive information required to provide access and improve the learning service. This may include your name, email address, login or access status, purchase status, test attempts, selected answers, scores, device information and basic technical logs.

Information provided directly

  • Details entered during sign-in, registration, purchase or support contact.
  • Messages submitted through the contact form.
  • Answers, test activity and progress created while using practice products.

Information collected automatically

  • Browser type, device type, timestamps and pages visited.
  • Technical information used for security, troubleshooting and performance.
  • Cookies or local browser storage used to maintain login and preferences.

2. How information is used

  • To activate and maintain authorised learning access.
  • To save progress, calculate results and show reviews.
  • To answer questions and resolve technical or access issues.
  • To prevent misuse, fraud and security incidents.
  • To improve content quality and platform reliability.

Kaushalya Academy does not sell personal information and does not use learner data to promise examination selection or employment.

3. Payments and third parties

Payments, authentication, hosting, analytics or database functions may be handled by third-party providers under their own terms and privacy practices. Kaushalya Academy does not intentionally store complete card or banking details on its learning pages.

External links

The website may link to another page or service. Kaushalya Academy is not responsible for the content, security or privacy practices of an external website after a learner leaves this platform.

4. Protection and retention

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect information, although no internet-based system can guarantee absolute security. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably required for access, support, accounting, legal, fraud-prevention or service-improvement purposes.

5. Your choices and policy updates

You may contact Kaushalya Academy to request correction or deletion of account-related information, subject to identity verification and applicable record-keeping requirements. Minors should use the platform with the knowledge of a parent or legal guardian.
